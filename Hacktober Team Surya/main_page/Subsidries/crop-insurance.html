Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY)

Overview

This crop insurance scheme provides farmers with financial protection against crop losses due to natural calamities such as floods, droughts, pests, and diseases.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Landholding farmers cultivating crops under the scheme.
  • Enrollment is through banks or local insurance providers.

Documents Required

  • Aadhaar Card.
  • Land ownership records.
  • Bank account details for the premium payment and insurance settlement.

Person to Approach

Farmers can approach banks, insurance companies, or Common Service Centers (CSCs) for enrolling in the Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana.

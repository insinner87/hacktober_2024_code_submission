Fertilizer Subsidy

Overview

The government provides subsidies on fertilizers to ensure farmers can access them at affordable prices.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Registered farmer purchasing for agricultural use.
  • Aadhaar-linked biometric verification required at PoS.

Documents Required

  • Aadhaar Card for authentication.
  • Land ownership proof if required.

Person to Approach

Visit registered fertilizer dealers or retailers equipped with PoS devices.

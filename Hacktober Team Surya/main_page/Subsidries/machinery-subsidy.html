Machinery and Equipment Subsidy

Overview

This subsidy program supports farmers by providing financial assistance to purchase modern machinery and equipment, such as tractors, harvesters, and seed drills, to increase farming efficiency.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Registered farmers eligible for machinery purchases.
  • Small and marginal farmers may receive preference in subsidy rates.

Documents Required

  • Land ownership records.
  • Identity proof (Aadhaar Card).
  • Quotation or invoice of the machinery being purchased.

Person to Approach

Contact your District Agriculture Office or State Agriculture Department for guidance and to submit your application for machinery subsidies.

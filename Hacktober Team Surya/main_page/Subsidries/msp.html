Minimum Support Price (MSP)

Overview

The Minimum Support Price (MSP) scheme guarantees farmers a minimum price for their crops, protecting them against sharp price drops in the market.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Farmers producing crops listed under the MSP program.
  • Registration with the local procurement agency is necessary.

Documents Required

  • Land ownership records.
  • Details of crop production.

Person to Approach

Farmers should visit their local procurement centers or government-designated Mandis to register and sell their crops under the MSP program.

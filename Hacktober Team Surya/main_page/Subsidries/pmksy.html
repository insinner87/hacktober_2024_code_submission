Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchai Yojana (PMKSY)

Overview

This scheme helps improve water-use efficiency through the installation of irrigation systems such as drip and sprinkler irrigation. Farmers receive subsidies to help reduce the cost of installation.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Landholding farmers with land registered for agricultural use.
  • Small and marginal farmers receive higher subsidies, but all are eligible.

Documents Required

  • Land ownership proof (Khasra-Khatauni).
  • Aadhaar Card or other identity proof.
  • Bank account details for subsidy transfer.
  • Details of the irrigation system (drip or sprinkler) to be installed.

Person to Approach

Visit your local Agriculture Department or District Agriculture Office to consult officials and submit the required documents for the irrigation subsidy.

Power Subsidy

Overview

Farmers can apply for reduced electricity rates for agricultural purposes. Subsidized electricity is offered for metered or unmetered agricultural power connections used for irrigation.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Farmers with a registered agricultural power connection.
  • Electricity usage restricted to agricultural purposes (e.g., irrigation).

Documents Required

  • Land ownership records.
  • Proof of identity (Aadhaar Card or other).
  • Application form for agricultural power connection.

Person to Approach

Contact your local electricity provider (DISCOM) or visit their nearest office to apply for an agricultural power connection and receive the subsidy.
